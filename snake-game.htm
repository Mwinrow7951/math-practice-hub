Snake Game

Snake Apple Game is one of the most famous classic games in the world. In this game, you control a snake that moves around the screen, eating food and growing longer. The goal is simple: make the snake as long as possible without crashing into the walls or your own tail.

The longer the snake grows, the harder the game becomes. Test your reflexes, focus, and speed in this fun online version of the Google Snake Game.

How to Play Snake Game

Start the game

  • Open the Classic Snake Game page and press Start.

  • Use the arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) on your keyboard or swipe on mobile to move.



Eat apples

  • Apples appear randomly.

  • Every time your snake eats one, it grows longer.

Avoid obstacles

  • Don’t hit the walls.

  • Don’t crash into your own body.

  • The game ends when the snake collides.


Score points

  • The more apples you eat, the higher your score.

  • Try to beat your personal best or challenge friends.
